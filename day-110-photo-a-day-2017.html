Day 110 - photo a day 2017

-

Posted on Thu 20 April 2017

This evening I decided to give my mega shock a long, long, long overdue strip down and proper service. I've had the seal/orings kit for quite a while but didn't have the tools for removal of the aip cap and the adapter to reinflate the shock. I built the cap removal tool and the adapter came in the post earlier this week.

All did not go to plan. No matter what I tried I could not get the air can to come apart. I tried strap wrenches, inner tubes and even tape + molgrips. Nope!
