Day 168 - photo a day 2017

-

Posted on Sat 17 June 2017

Today I went for a ride in the Dyfi, I wanted to ride all of the descents that I know of on the Corris side. I started off with footpath and my balance was pretty screwy, it kind of set the tone for the rest of the ride. I didn't feel comfortable on any descents and my confidence was just gone, guess balance being iffy played a part in that... In the end I skipped 3 descents (still managed 6) but discovered a new trail. I didn't do the other 3 as I just wasn't really enjoying myself all that much. Ho hum! I also didn't take a photo... However, some chillies are growing nicely now on this plant and I have 1 growing on the other plant, that one looks kinda cool - it's a bruised colour.
