Day 5 - photo a day

-

Posted on Thu 05 January 2017

Today Elysia left her job so was feeling a bit down, I decided that I'd take her out for some food. We went to Casablanca, a Morrocan restaurant. The food and service were both really great, it's well worth a visit if you're in Aberystwyth. We had hummus to share and then I had the Seafood Provencal, she had the Lamb Koftas. We were both suitably stuffed afterward.
