Today I went to ride the masochists trail at myherin with Ben, Rich, Bean and Steph. It was great, the trails were running well and even the tech climbs seem to have been smoothed out a bit. The weather was cold but dry, there were patches dusted with snow. The ebike meant that I was able to get up all of the climbs on eco mode.
