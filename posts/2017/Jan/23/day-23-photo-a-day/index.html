Day 23 - photo a day

Posted on Mon 23 January 2017

How better to finish off a three day weekend than a ride in the Dyfi forest? I headed over with Bean and Steph today and we rode some of our favourites on the Corris side. The trails were running well despite it raining last night and one of them having a couple of riders on it yesterday. The weather was also really nice, a bit warmer than yesterday and mostly sunny.
