Day 82 - photo a day

-

Posted on Thu 23 March 2017

Elysia is off to Japan for two weeks and got the 5:30am train this morning so I was up to take her to the station. I managed to get another hour of sleep once I got home and I was in work for 8 on the dot. I was meant to go with her but at time of booking flights I was struggling with my bladder and hadn't yet worked out how to manage it very well. This made me somewhat reluctant to spend hours on a train and then 2 weeks in a foreign country where they speak a different language. She's also gone with another friend anyway so she's not alone. I'm hoping to go next time for sure though.
