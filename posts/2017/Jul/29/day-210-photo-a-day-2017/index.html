Day 210 - photo a day 2017

-

Posted on Sat 29 July 2017

This is basically how my gamejam has gone! For one reason or another it's only been myself and Elysia doing it and she's been at work today. I'm not a big fan of the theme - "Running out of power". We didn't want to do something generic so we decided that running out of power could be a metaphor for life getting harder or something like that. We had an idea where you play the day to day life of someone with MS, starting healthy and then ending up in a different state - varying from healthy to not very healthy. I was struggling to work out how to make this into a game, and also how to respect MS and not accidentally make it seem trivial in some way. Defold editor also kept maxing out my RAM at random! In the end I gave up and played Borderlands 2 for the rest of the day!
