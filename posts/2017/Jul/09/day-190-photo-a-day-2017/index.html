Day 190 - photo a day 2017

-

Posted on Sun 09 July 2017

Legs weren't feeling great so I decided to go out on my bike for an easy one. That somehow changed very rapidly into a bit of a bigger ride than I had planned and using eco mode on the bike. In the end I rode up to Angler's Retreat via Zorro. I very, very nearly managed the Nant-y-cagal climb but a balance mistake saw me nearly go back down the step bit over the bars instead. It was really great to ride Hockey Mask again, it's awesome. I also regained a load of my confidence for some reason. All in all, a really great ride!
