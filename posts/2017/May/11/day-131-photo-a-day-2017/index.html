Day 131 - photo a day 2017

-

Posted on Thu 11 May 2017

Nothing exciting yet again. Had hoped to get my bike back as Specialized say they've fixed it, maybe tomorrow. I think over the last 5 weeks it's been in my possession for 3 or 4 days. We've also had glorious weather the last couple of weeks and now it appears that the weather is turning for the foreseeable. I'm quite disappointed about it but not much to be done.

As it's been an uneventful day I'm falling back to my list of things to photo when I have no ideas. So today is of my meds, Tecfidera (dimethyl fumarate). I think that they're doing the job as I've had no relapses and I don't think that I have any side effects from them either. I certainly haven't contracted PML which is a rare but worrying side effect. The MS team monitor my blood levels for any sign of activity that lead to contracting it so I'm not really bothered about it though. I have an appointment with my neurologist next week so we'll see what he thinks. I'm also going to see what he thinks about the overcomingms diet, that could be interesting!
