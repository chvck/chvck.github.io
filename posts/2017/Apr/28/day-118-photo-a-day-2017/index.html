Day 118 - photo a day 2017

-

Posted on Fri 28 April 2017

This weekend coming is the Dyfi Enduro. A big ride with over 1000 other riders in the Dyfi forest, mass start in Machynlleth. I thought that it was probably wise to give my bike a service beforehand. I managed to replace the really worn bottom bracket with a slightly worn bottom bracket, fixed a creak with my saddle, and refreshed the oil in my fork lowers and damper cart. The oil that came out of the damper cart was white, that's not so good. Definitely overdue! Anyway, bike is running about as well as it's going to...
