Day 47 - photo a day

Published: Fri 17 February 2017
By Charles Dixon

In misc.

tags: riding photo-a-day-2017

Translations: en
This morning I was sat in work looking out wishing I was riding my bike, the weather was great. I decided that I was going out after work for sure, I've not ridden much lately and the feeling that I need to is returning. By lunch I was a bit less enthusiastic and when I knocked off at 4 it was starting to rain. I decided that was going out for some form of ride regardless, even if only for an hour. I could do a road ride on the ebike. I got home and changed and decided that I couldn't be bothered fitted battery etc... so went out on my roadrat for an hour and a half. It rained a bit, I got a bit wet, but it felt good to be out and I got in 1.5 hours. It ws only a short ride, and quite slow. My cardio fitness feels fine really but that might be because my legs won't let me push myself much. Hills were no problem though, just a bit slow!
