Day 40 - photo a day

Posted on Fri 10 February 2017

Elysia and I went out for food tonight, we'd both wanted to go to Pystgoty fish restaurant for a while so we finally got round to booking and went. I knew that the place was small but it really is tiny! Four two person tables in a row is the lot. Although the tables are all in a row and the place is small you do still feel like you have your own space and some privacy. The toilet is also actually a completely different building - they use the public toilets a couple of buildings down! The food was fantastic, on the steep side price wise but worth it. I had fish soup and hake bake and those were both tasty, I probably could have just eaten the fish soup all night it was amazing. Anyway, here's a hake bake.
