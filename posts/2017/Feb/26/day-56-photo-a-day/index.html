Day 56 - photo a day

-

Posted on Sun 26 February 2017

The weather today wasn't great but I decided to ride anyway. I wanted to give my legs a bit of a test so decided to ride the mega again. I drove up to Nant to do a bit of a loop. I ended up doing a Pendam loop plus the moorlands climb (and then back down the climb), on reaching the top of the climb I decided that it probably wasn't a good idea to try ride Zorro and the leg burner. I think I made the right choice, my legs were feeling pretty weak by the time I got to high as a kite. Still, was nice to get out and that bike feels great to ride as it's so light (relatively)!
