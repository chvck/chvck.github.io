Published: Sat 25 February 2017
By Charles Dixon

In misc.

tags: photo-a-day-2017

Today I went back to near Oswestry for a meal at Plas yn dinas in Llanfechain with my family and my Dad's ladyfriend's family so that everyone could meet one another (Elysia came with me too). It was good to meet them and they seem really nice. The food was great, I had fish cakes and then tempura battered sea bass. Would recommend for sure.
