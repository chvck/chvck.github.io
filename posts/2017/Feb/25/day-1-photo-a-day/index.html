Published: Sat 25 February 2017
By Charles Dixon

In misc.

tags: photo-a-day-2017

Translations: en
Another year, another attempt. Hopefully I'll do a bit better this year but I doubt it!

As it's new years day I decided to go for a ride. It's been a pretty warm day although quite a lot of wind, and the wind is coooold. Had a fun ride and mostly stayed dry too. i bumped into someone I work with and had to turn the bike to turbo just to keep up with him so that was a decent workout and good to spend some of the ride chatting. Got a few PRs and only nearly crashed a couple of times so pretty good! This photo is one I've taken many, many times now but it's still a view that I really like.
