Day 181 - photo a day 2017

-

Posted on Fri 30 June 2017

The final gravity of this batch of beer has settled at around 1.005 putting the percentage at 6-6.5%. Last week's weather has likely played hell with the yeast as the fermentation got much warmer than it should have. As it's settled I decided that I'd best siphon it into my plastic keg as I've run out of bottles. Sampling it there's plenty of sediment in it and the taste isn't bad but it isn't good either. It'll spend another in the airing cupboard and then it's going into the garage for a month so we'll see how it turns out...
