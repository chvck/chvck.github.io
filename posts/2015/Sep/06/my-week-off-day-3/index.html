My week off - Day 3

Published: Sun 06 September 2015
By Charles Dixon

In misc.

tags: riding

Today was supposed to be the woods and Nant. Taylor said that he wouldn't be around until a bit late on so we decided we'd do a nant loop. It took me until around 3pm to actually get going and do anything, I went to the woods and had a rad couple of hours. Taylor rang me to say that he'd be too late for a ride so I just stayed a bit longer than I'd planned. I rode the same short section about 11 times it was so good!

Today I stayed over the side with the main lines on it and didn't venture over the other side, I spent most of my time riding the top sections trying to get them fast. I also decided to try out both lines on the dual slalom track, I stacked it on both. More slippery than a slippery thing!

Believe it or not but it took me 11 attempts to get round this corner cleanly, even then it wasn't fast...
