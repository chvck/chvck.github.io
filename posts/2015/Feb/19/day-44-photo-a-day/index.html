Day 44 - Photo a Day

Published: Thu 19 February 2015
By Charles Dixon

In misc.

tags: photo-a-day-2015

Translations: en

Today I decided to go and explore a couple of bridleways up nant, ones I'd not done before.

The first new one linked the highest bit of the mountain road down to the bont goch climb, making more climb before getting to nant. It started off downhill on a double track before turning into a single track. It was almost like whoops but shorter and begging to catch the front wheel. It then had a short grassy climb before an open grassy descent to the other climb. I managed to do over 500m climb by the time I got to hippity hop.

Next I headed out on the Joe's back garden loop as far as the reservoir. I then followed the road round and rode the splashdown climb to angler's, by this point I was a bit worried about time before dark so went straight for the bridleway. All that I can really say about it is that the track isn't well defined and wasn't great! Oh well.
