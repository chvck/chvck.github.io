Day 41 - Photo a Day

-

Posted on Wed 11 February 2015

When I left aber today it was cold and foggy. It continued that way all the way up through Banc y darren but pretty much as soon as I reached the Nant area the clouds parted and the sun came out to play! I stopped at the cafe for a, massive, wedge of cake and a cup of coffee - very nice. I then decided to go out on the Syfydrin trail where I got some amazing views over the lakes, a combination of fog and sun. After the first part of the Bont Goch descent, at the gate, I stopped and realised that there was complete silence. Not a sound to be heard, other than the ringing in my ears - didn't realise how bad that was! It was quite an awesome moment and really reminded me how important it was that I didn't crash out there. Rad ride.
