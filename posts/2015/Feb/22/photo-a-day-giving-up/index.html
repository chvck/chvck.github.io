Photo a Day - Giving up

-

Posted on Sun 22 February 2015

I've decided to give up on the photo a day, day 50 seemed like a good milestone to stop at. I wanted to try make each photo have a bit of meaning/story to it and that was starting to become harder and harder, not to mention that I didn't always manage that in what I did actually do. I could see trying to find a photo getting closer and closer to arty photos of the inside of the microwave and I didn't want that so felt it time to bring it to a stop.
