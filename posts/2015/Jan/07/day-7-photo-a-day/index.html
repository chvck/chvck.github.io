Day 7 - Photo a Day

-

Posted on Wed 07 January 2015

Beginning to feel better, hurrah! Been waiting for a package to arrive all day today, wish it was with DPD - I'd know what time it was coming then. Anyway, today's photo is of the state of my rear lawn. It doesn't look great does it? Puddles, big blue bins and a bit of a mess in the part that isn't lawn. My plan (before the ground gets dry) is to dig up the lawn level with the garage and clear out the part that already isn't lawn. This will give me an area to make a bit of a vegetable garden and a proper compost heap, which means that the big blue bins can be removed. I might use them for water butts but I'm not convinced that I'll use that much water in the garden. I intend on digging up the slabs, that presently exist as puddle bases, and I'll use them in the vegetable patch. I'll use some of the dug up lawn to replace the missing patches of grass that removing the slabs will leave. The washing line pole and fence could also do with a bit of paint. Big plans, time shall tell how well I get on - with any luck there will be a photo later in the year with it all looking better and some vegetables growing!
