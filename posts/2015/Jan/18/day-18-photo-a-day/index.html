Day 18 - Photo a Day

-

Posted on Sun 18 January 2015

Today's photo is from the Banc-y-darren road looking out toward the mountain road and the sea. I cheated a bit for today's rideout by parking at Pwll Crwn which meant that I didn't have to climb back up to the Waun at the end of the ride. I bumped into some Ystwyth MTB crew at the bottom of the leg burner and again at the top and had a bit of a catchup and a good chat. The ride was great and the only hiccup was a crank arm falling off down white fridge, sketchy as! I refitted it with a rock, all good.
