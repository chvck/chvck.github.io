Day 11 - Photo a Day

-

Posted on Sun 11 January 2015

Yesterday I did a ride out to Nant and felt absolutely fine until I really didn't. I was feeling quite good but taking it a little bit easy when I suddenly just faded, my legs still had energy but I just felt really tired. I still did the complete ride (I did skip one descent) and pedalled everything fine but felt terrible, even got a 2015 KOM on the top of white fridge. When I got home I pretty much collapsed onto my bed (after taking off muddy kit!) for half an hour, and didn't feel better for hours. Strange.

This meant that I wasn't keen on riding today and instead decided to let my body rest. I did some work on my big bike and did some painting before going for a bit of walk in Gogerddan woods. I currently go running (not regularly admittedly) in Cwm Pwll woods but they're very small, about 1km per lap and totally flat. My explore of Gogerddan turned up a waymarked walking route that I hadn't expected. It's around 2.5km long and does have a hill in it, so looks good for running. It is a bit short but should do the job! The top photo is my photo a day for today and the others are just a bonus.
