Day 14 - Photo a Day

Published: Wed 14 January 2015
By Charles Dixon

In misc.

tags: photo-a-day-2015

Translations: en

This evening I went up to Nant with the big bike for a short spin. The weather was really quite terrible! It was raining and the wind was unreal, I spent most of the ride unable to see because of the rain on my glasses and them steaming up slightly. On the descents I spent most of the time trying to not get blown off the trail by the wind, I sat down for most of High as a Kite the wind was so strong! None-the-less I enjoyed myself and it reminded me how much fun my big bike is.
