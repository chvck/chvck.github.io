Day 3 - Photo a Day

Published: Sat 03 January 2015
By Charles Dixon

In misc.

tags: photo-a-day-2015

Translations: en

Still ill. Luckily, my first The Spicery box arrived today. I got given a subscription for xmas from my family. The idea is that each month you get sent a box with the spices for a meal, you buy the remaining ingredients and make the meal. There's a choice of "Friday Night Curry", "World Kitchen" and "Date Night". For me this was a choice of World Kitchen and Date Night, I have enough curry spices as it is! Despite being single I went for Date Night as it's a 3 course meal for 2, so it'll do me for 2 nights.

This month's box is Sri Lanka, the menu is: Tomato rasam, coconut and fish curry and love cake. I'm quite excited to make this meal, it's going to have to wait a week though! It also came with a little detox spices sachet, the idea of these spices is a simple dish full of spices to aid with digestion. The 2 dishes they suggest are a lassi and a dahl, I'll be trying the dahl for lunch.
