Day 1 - Photo a Day

Published: Thu 01 January 2015
By Charles Dixon

In misc.

tags: photo-a-day-2015

Translations: en

A new year has rolled around and once again I've decided to attempt a photo a day. I strongly suspect that before January ends that I'll be doing a photo a week but hopefully this'll drive me to get out more and do some interesting things.

So, day 1. I'm quite ill with this flu that's doing the rounds. Typically on the 1st January I like to go riding but that isn't about to happen, I feel better today than yesterday but want to get rid of this ASAP. As such, today's photo is of a bit of bike fettling - bleeding the brakes on my BFe. Reminds me, that I really need to sort out my garage... With any luck I'll also be taking better quality photos as time goes by!
