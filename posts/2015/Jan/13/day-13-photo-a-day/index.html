Day 13 - Photo a Day

Posted on Tue 13 January 2015

Today's photo is the view from my bedroom window this morning when I opened my blind. Exciting stuff eh? Sun in Winter is always good, part of the reason for choosing this photo is what happened with the rest of the day. By 9 it was tipping down, by 10 it was snowing. The snow persisted for a few hours and then blue skies! That was followed by hail before going dark but dry again. I decided to drive up to Nant for a ride and at the top of the mountain road it was, once again, snowing and my car was showing it as -0.5C. I was somewhat concerned that driving back down the mountain road later on would not be great so bailed and just came home. It's just been hailing again.
