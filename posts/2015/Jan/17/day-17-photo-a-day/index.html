Day 17 - Photo a Day

-

Posted on Sat 17 January 2015

tl;dr the postie arrived so I went riding...

Looking out to the sea from the forest car park on the mountain road up the Nant. I fitted my 10 speed kit to my hardtail this morning and then went back to bed as I felt ill. I was about to write off the day when the doorbell rang forcing me out of bed, post man! He bought me coffee beans so I decided to have a coffee and a bacon sandwich and stop feeling sorry for myself. I had left it too late to get a full ride out in the light done so drove up to the forestry car park and did a Summit loop from there. I really enjoyed the ride and definitely feel recovered, although a bit slow, now. Also reclaimed my 2015 KOM on White Fridge back from Dave! Not an overall PR but only 2s off, I'll get sub 30s eventually.
