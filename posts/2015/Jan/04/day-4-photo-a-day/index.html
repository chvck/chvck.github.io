Day 4 - Photo a Day

Posted on Sun 04 January 2015

Today I was due to ride the Red Kite Frozen Devil but I made a call yesterday that I was going to have to bail due to my cold. I arranged to do a bit of nant ride with Andy as he was over this way surfing this morning. I also really, really wanted to show him toboggan. We started the ride with it and, as I expected, he had no problems and thought it was great. The rest of the ride was quite good fun but I was taking it very easy trying not to get out of breath and make my chest hurt too much. It hurt a lot anyway and I generally felt quite rubbish so I skipped High as a Kite and Hippity Hop. So, today's photo is Andy looking out down the valley with a few red kites floating around.
