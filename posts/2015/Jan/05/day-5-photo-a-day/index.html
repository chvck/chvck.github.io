Day 5 - Photo a Day -

Posted on Mon 05 January 2015

First day back at work this year and, as expected, first day when I've been a little lost what to photo. I'm still keen to not just take photos or random stuff and want things that I can write a bit of something about. So today is an indiscriminate bottle of clear liquid. I joke, it's a bottle of the beer that I recently brewed. After seeing someone recommend it online I used a Evil Dog Double IPA kit as I wanted to ensure that what I brewed tasted drinkable. The procedure was quite simple -

mix in bucket, put somewhere warm, siphon to bottles. The beer has come clear quite nicely but once the cap comes off it goes really cloudy. Not sure why! The bottles will be moved from the house to the garage soon to see if being cool helps with that. The beer itself tastes quite nice although tastes quite strong as well. It's very drinkable!
