Day 31 - Photo a Day

Posted on Sat 31 January 2015

Hurrah I've managed a whole month of photos! Today I went for a shortish ride up Nant, just toboggan and then over to the main trails and did a Pendam loop. First ride on the 44s and, wow, they're properly nice. Had to back the rebound off a touch as compressing the forks always wound up with the front wheel in the air but they're like riding on a magic carpet. Very happy with them. I managed to take a few photos today and had all sorts of weather but my choice as my photo for the day is of this bird. It was flitting around my feet eating the bits of cake that I dropped, pretty rad!
