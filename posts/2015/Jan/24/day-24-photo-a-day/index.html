Day 24 - Photo a Day

Published: Sat 24 January 2015
By Charles Dixon

In misc.

tags: photo-a-day-2015

Translations: en

It's Dyfi winter warmup weekend! Today I picked up Zop from the station and cracked on up to Nant to meet up with Andy and Taylor, although Andy isn't actually riding tomorrow. Turns out that Taylor also doesn't have a ticket, skills. He's going to try talk nicely to the event organisers though! The three of them rode reaper whilst I went and did a trail that runs down near Mark of Zorro. I got down in time to see them coming down the scree and this photo is of Taylor and Zop on the steep scree corner.
