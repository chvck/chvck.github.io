Day 25 - Photo a Day

Published: Sun 25 January 2015
By Charles Dixon

In misc.

tags: photo-a-day-2015

Translations: en

Today was the Dyfi Winter Warmup. We left around 9:30 to give Taylor time to try buy a ticket, luckily he was able to. We got there around 10:30 and signed on then we started riding at around 11. It was a rad event and we managed 2 very sociable laps. The loop was really good and took in Builth and 3in1 which are Dyfi classics, the first lap also went down Elephant. On the second lap we did a cheeky little loamy trail that runs alongside part of 3in1 which was really rad too. Top day out and saw loads of familiar faces!
