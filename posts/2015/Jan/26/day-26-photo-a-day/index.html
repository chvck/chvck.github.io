Day 26 - Photo a Day

Posted on Mon 26 January 2015

This morning I went for a ride round Nant with Bean, bit of a spin down after the winter warmup. I had a job interview in the afternoon so I didn't want to ride anything too wild! Got offered the job just for reference :). We did a standard YstwythMTB loop so around 25km, it was a fun ride and nice to have a good catchup too. The ride had one downer in that White Fridge is currently unridable due to logging, gutted. Hopefully it won't be too hard to rescue once they remove the logs and hopefully they don't log the full trail.
