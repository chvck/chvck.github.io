Being away from home and spending all of my time in the office or where I'm staying is making a photo a bit more difficult. Today we have a photo of my Toshiba Chromebook. It's a good little machine. Boots fast and I like ChromeOS. Aside from the trackpad it also feels more expensive than it is. The only main complaint I have with it is that the fan gets quite loud. I assumed that this was a fault and sent it off on warranty but was stuck with a bill as they marked it as no fault found - guess the volume meets their acceptable limits but not mine.
