Day 28 - Photo a Day

Published: Wed 28 January 2015
By Charles Dixon

In misc.

tags: photo-a-day-2015

Translations: en

Christmas Eve I had a stack in the Dyfi and whilst correcting my bars Andy noticed that my bottom headset cup was very loose, most likely borked frame. Turns out that Stu, his brother, had an identical frame that I could buy... Whilst at it Andy could sell me his Mazrzocchi 44 RC2Ti forks n all. I said to get me a price and we'd see, well the price he gave me was less than a new frame + headset so we went ahead with the deal. Today I got round to starting to move all the bits over to the new frame and fit the new forks, they're shiny! Turns out that I can also fit and remove the bottom cup from the old frame by hand...
