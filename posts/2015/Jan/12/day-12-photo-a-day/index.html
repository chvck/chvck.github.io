Day 12 - Photo a Day

Published: Mon 12 January 2015
By Charles Dixon

In misc.

tags: photo-a-day-2015

Translations: en

Today's photo isn't hugely inspired but it's of my bird feeders, with a Great Tit on one and a Blackbird on the ground. It seems that the birds really prefer the sunflower hearts to the mixed seeds. My office (not where the photo was taken) looks out with the feeders straight ahead of me and I really like watching the birds, they provide an occasional nice distraction from work. The peg basket really needs to go though!
