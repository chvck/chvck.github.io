Day 15 - Photo a Day

Published: Thu 15 January 2015
By Charles Dixon

In misc.

tags: photo-a-day-2015

Translations: en

Given the horrendous wind that we're getting at the moment, along with intermittent hail today, I decided to forego a ride this evening. Instead, I went for a run at lunch time, my first in Gogerddan woods. It wasn't long at 2.5km but the steep hill ensured that it still took me 20-25 minutes and a bit of walking! The photo is looking inland, somewhere sunnier, from the top of the hill.
