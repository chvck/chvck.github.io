My week off - Day 2

Posted on Mon 31 August 2015

I spent around 4 hours today in the woods, half of that was probably working on my new track. I've got one fallen tree left to take out and a bit of dirt to pile on other and the top half of it'll be ridable. I need to clear a bit of undergrowth and then properly cut in a rut and it'll be done. It works well as the push up as is, much easier than pushing up the other track and should open up scope for building a load of other tracks more easily.

After building I rode the steep line a couple of times and then the top of Nick's into the rock garden track. Wasn't many runs but my legs were pretty tired from the building so I left it at that, the slickness was also scaring me a little!
