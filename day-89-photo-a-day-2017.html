Day 89 - photo a day

-

Posted on Thu 30 March 2017

I wasn't feeling great today but I had a package due to be delivered there so headed in to work. In the end I took the afternoon off as the package was delivered. The package - some new pedals. Spank spoons. They're nice and big at 110x100mm, they actually come in 3 sizes; 110, 100, 90 for different sized feet. I'm not entirely sure why they come without the pins fitted but they do come with an allen key, wrench, and pins. I'm looking forward to pedals that don't rattle, squeak and aren't missing 2 pins that can't be replaced due to the thread being stripped!
