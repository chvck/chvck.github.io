Day 68 - photo a day

-

Posted on Thu 09 March 2017

I'm not entirely sure what came over me but when I got home from work I decided to start work on sorting out the rear patio area. There are currently a few shrubs, 2 gravel patches and 2 trees all which I want to take out. OK, I'm not going to take out the trees I'm just going to cut them down quite low on the trunk. In place of all of these things I'm going to plant various herbs, I want the trees gone to try open things up a bit as they're a bit commandeering.

So this evening I started by removing the biggest of the shrubs which took me a good couple of hours and now my garden waste bag is full again. First job done though, woo!
