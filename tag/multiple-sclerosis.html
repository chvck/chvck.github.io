Day 45 - photo a day

Published: Tue 14 February 2017
By Charles Dixon

In misc.

tags: multiple-sclerosis photo-a-day-2017

Translations: en
This one isn't a happy fun time photo but part of the point of this was as a partial ongoing log. It's an incontinence bed protector pad thing. I've mostly got bed wetting under control but now and then it happens. It's happened twice in last week (first time this year) and one of those times I really can't account for why. The advantage of this thing is that I can also take it with me when I stay away from home and not have to sleep on top of a quite narrow bivvy bag as a safeguard. I'll still have to sleep in my sleeping bag to avoid soiling (is that the right word for urine?) the bedsheets but it's something.

Edit: well that's now 3 times this week, time to monitor drink intake and be more regimented. No caffeine in the afternoon and no drinks after 8...
