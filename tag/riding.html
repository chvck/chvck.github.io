Published: Sun 19 February 2017

tags: riding photo-a-day-2017

Translations: en
Today has been quite glorious weather so I decided this morning that I was going riding. I hadn't fully planned on where just that I might try to get out for a bit of a longish one. I rode a couple of things that I don't normally ride and I remembered to continue avoiding the one of them; rutty, boggy mess. Managed to get in a few river crossings too. I did 57.5km with 2450m elevation gain so happy enough!
